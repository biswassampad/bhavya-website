Museum

At Kala Bhoomi, we aspire to be more than just a collection of unique objects. Thus we are devoted to creating a dynamic and interactive learning experience.

Collection

The Handicrafts galleries are on Terracotta, Traditional Paintings, Stone and Wood carving, Natural material crafts and Tribal Crafts. The Handlooms galleries display unique and antique raw materials and equipment as well as variety of Handlooms from across the state

Workshops

At Kala Bhoomi we aspire to be more than simply a collection of unique objects. If you like being exposed to other cultures and interacting with locals, then look no further than this. Feel free to enjoy our authentic workshops, one craft at a time.

Exhibitions

At Kala Bhoomi, we aspire to make our artefacts come alive. Unique exhibitions and evolving displays constantly keeps the museum interesting while our team of curators are always on the lookout for new data and stories. With over 50 different types of Handicrafts and Handlooms at display our exhibitions are ever changing and fresh!

Museum Trails

Museum trails are an important activity to create awareness among people about the history and culture of Odisha. Through a museum trail association with available historic settlements and environment can be developed. It motivates tourists, and local population to visit rich cultural/ archaeological/ architectural sites. These experiences also have the capacity to sensitize local populations and in turn to preserve and conserve their own culture and heritage.
